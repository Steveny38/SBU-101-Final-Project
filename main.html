    Problem Statement

    Our aim is to support domestic violence survivors in their recovery while raising awareness and fighting agaisnt the ongoing spread of violence.

    Overview

    Domestic violence involves abusive behaviors within family and intimate relationships. It is a pervasive issue that affects individuals across diverse demographics regardless of age, gender, and cultural background. Consequences of domestic violence can lead to physical injuries, emotional trauma, and long-term psychological effects for victims. With a combination of support services and public awareness campaigns, we aim to aid those affected and minimize its impact.
